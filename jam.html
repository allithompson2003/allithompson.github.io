SIMPLY CITRUS

PACKAGING / ILLUSTRATION

The original concept of 'Simply Citrus' was thought of by myself.

'Simply Citrus' is a citrus–based jam company owned by the family brand Jade Eyes. They hope curious foodies in their 20s will purchase and love their sweet and sour jam products.

I designed the package in Adobe Illustrator and created the illustrations using Procreate.

The mockup was sourced online.
