BOWL'D

BRANDING / LOGO DESIGN / PACKAGING

Alli Thompson is a student in her second year in the Graphic Design program at Conestoga College. Her work is rooted in creating bold and fun designs that enhance and build identities. Alli’s strong interest in branding and logo design was recognized in 2022 when she was awarded the RGD first–year Artista Award for logo design and brand identity. She continues to learn new skills and keep up with the latest design trends.
